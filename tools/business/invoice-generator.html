Do freelancers in India need a GST invoice?

Whether you need to charge GST depends on your annual turnover and business structure. Freelancers with turnover below the GST registration threshold (currently ₹20 lakh for services in most states, ₹10 lakh in special category states) generally aren't required to register for GST or charge it, and can issue a simple Non-GST invoice instead. Once you cross the threshold or voluntarily register, you're required to charge GST and include your GSTIN on every invoice.

A proper invoice — whether GST or Non-GST — should include a unique invoice number, the date, both parties' details, a clear breakdown of services rendered, and the total amount due. This isn't just good practice for getting paid on time; it's also the record you'll need for tax filing and, if a client disputes payment, proof of the work agreement.

What GST rate should I use for freelance/consulting services?

Most professional and consulting services fall under 18% GST, but rates can vary by service category. Confirm the applicable rate for your specific service with a tax professional or the GST portal.

Is this invoice legally valid for tax filing?

This tool generates a properly formatted invoice document, but Claude — and this tool — can't confirm tax or legal compliance for your specific situation. Consult a chartered accountant for GST registration and filing requirements.
