yt --thumbnail --hd

YouTube Thumbnail Extractor

Paste any YouTube video URL to instantly preview and download its thumbnail in multiple resolutions, including Full HD 1080p (when available).

Why download YouTube thumbnails directly?

Every YouTube video automatically generates a set of thumbnail images at different resolutions, hosted directly on YouTube's own image servers. Creators often need quick access to these — for making a reaction thumbnail, building a video roundup graphic, or referencing a competitor's thumbnail style for design research. Instead of taking a screenshot (which loses quality and often includes the play button overlay), this tool links straight to the original hosted image.

YouTube serves several thumbnail sizes for every video — from a small 120px preview up to the 1280×720 "maxresdefault" version — but not every video has the higher resolutions available, since it depends on the original upload quality and how the creator set their thumbnail. This tool shows every available size so you can pick the highest quality one that actually exists for that video.

Why is the HD thumbnail missing for some videos?

The 1280×720 "maxresdefault" thumbnail isn't generated for every video — mainly older uploads or videos under a certain resolution. In that case, use the next best size shown (like standard or high quality).

Is this tool downloading anything from YouTube's private servers?

No — it only links to YouTube's publicly accessible thumbnail image URLs, which are the same images visible in search results and video previews.
